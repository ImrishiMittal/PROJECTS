Geographical Location of Lakshadweep

Lakshadweep is an archipelago of 36 islands located in the Arabian Sea, off the southwestern coast of India. The islands are spread over an area of about 30,000 square miles in the Arabian Sea, though the land area is only around 30 square kilometers. It lies approximately 200 to 400 kilometers off the coast of Kerala, India, making it the smallest Union Territory of India both in terms of area and population.

The archipelago enjoys abundant sunshine with occasional rainfalls and a moderate breeze.
